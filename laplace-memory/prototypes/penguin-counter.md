# Penguin Counter

## Summary
Prototype concept for counting penguins in an uploaded photograph.

## Domain
Computer vision, wildlife monitoring

## Task Type
Vision counting

## Inputs
Single image upload.

## Output
Estimated penguin count, confidence, explanation, and warnings.

## Approach
Use an external foundation vision model from a server-side API route.

## Reuse Notes
Reusable for other object-counting demos such as animals, vehicles, people, or field assets.

## Limitations
Foundation vision models may produce approximate counts and may be less reliable on crowded or low-quality images.
